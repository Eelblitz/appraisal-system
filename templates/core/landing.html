What We Offer

Everything Your Ministry Needs

One platform that handles the entire GEN 79 appraisal process — from HR setup to employee PDF download.

Multi-Ministry SaaS

Multiple ministries and MDAs use one platform simultaneously with complete data isolation. Ministry of Finance cannot see Ministry of Health's data — ever.

Complete GEN 79 Workflow

All four parts of the annual performance evaluation form — digitised, structured, and sequentially enforced. No part can be skipped or filled out of order.

Role-Based Access

Five distinct roles — HR Admin, Reporting Officer, Countersigning Officer, Employee, and Platform Admin. Each role sees only what is relevant to them.

Paystack Payment Integration

Employees pay a token fee set by HR to download their completed PDF. Revenue is automatically split between the platform and your organisation.

Professional PDF Reports

Official evaluation reports are generated on-the-fly with your organisation's logo, all four parts included, and proper signature areas — ready to print or archive.

Secure and Auditable

Every action is tracked — who filled what, when it was submitted, and how many times a PDF was downloaded. Full audit trail for compliance and accountability.

The Process

How It Works

From HR setup to employee PDF download — the complete appraisal cycle in four steps.

1
HR Admin
Setup and Assign

HR creates the appraisal cycle, sets the download fee, builds a template with performance aspects, then assigns employees in bulk by department.

2
Employee
Fill Part 1

Employee logs in, fills their personal records, job description and main duties. They can save drafts and return before final submission.

3
Reporting Officer
Assess Performance

Reporting Officer rates the employee on each performance aspect (A to E), selects an overall rating, and completes the training and promotability assessment.

4
Countersigning Officer
Sign Off and Download

Countersigning Officer provides final sign-off. Employee is notified, pays the download fee, and receives their official evaluation PDF report.

The Federal Ministry of Finance cannot see the Federal Ministry of Health's data, employees, or appraisals — even though they share the same platform. Data isolation is enforced at the database level on every single query.

The entire GEN 79 Annual Performance Evaluation form is digitised into four distinct parts, each filled by the appropriate officer. The system enforces the correct sequence — an officer cannot fill Part 2 until the employee submits Part 1. The generated PDF report contains all four parts in a professional format with your organisation's branding.

HR Admin sets a download fee per appraisal cycle (e.g. ₦2,000). When an employee pays to download their completed PDF, the platform automatically calculates the revenue split. A configurable percentage goes to the platform (Code With Iman) and the remainder stays with your organisation. All payments are processed securely through Paystack. A full payment report is available on the HR Admin dashboard.

Yes. The system comes pre-loaded with all 16 standard GEN 79 performance aspects. HR Admin can edit the descriptions of existing aspects, deactivate aspects that are not relevant to their organisation (e.g. "Management of Staff" for junior officers), and add custom aspects unique to their organisation. Different staff categories can use different sets of aspects through the template system.

Officers can save drafts at any point and return to complete the form later.
